Who I Am

About Nadav Nir

Freelance Sound Engineer, Formerly Broadcasting Engineering Supervisor at KAN - the Israeli Public Broadcasting Corporation. BA (Hons) in Sound Technology from LIPA, Liverpool. Based in Tel Aviv.

My Story

I'm a Freelance Sound Engineer, formerly working as a Broadcasting Engineering Supervisor at KAN - the Israeli Public Broadcasting Corporation in Jerusalem. In 2016 I graduated with a BA (Hons) in Sound Technology from LIPA (Liverpool Institute for Performing Arts, UK).

In my day-to-day work I operate consoles, calibrate RF devices, manage signal processors and speaker PA systems across broadcast and live environments. I also hold a Cyber Security & Cloud Computing diploma from the Technion (2023).

Beyond engineering I'm a guitarist and music creator - always glad to bring technical and musical interests together when relevant.

What I Offer

Services

Audio services tailored to your project's needs - from studio sessions to broadcast-ready deliverables.

Studio Recording

Full-service recording sessions for solo artists, bands, ensembles, and voice talent.

  • Multi-track recording & session management
  • Microphone placement & signal chain optimisation
  • Real-time monitoring & artist communication
  • Instrument & vocal tuning

Mixing

Creative and technical mixing that balances each element of your production to bring out clarity, depth, and emotion.

  • Stereo & immersive (Atmos) mixing
  • Stem mixing & stems delivery
  • Parallel processing & bus routing
  • Revision rounds included

Mastering

Final polish and loudness optimisation ensuring your music translates perfectly across all playback systems and streaming platforms.

  • Streaming-optimised loudness (LUFS)
  • Vinyl & CD mastering
  • Reference comparison
  • Multiple format delivery

Broadcast Audio

Broadcast-grade audio production for television, radio, streaming, and live events - meeting all technical compliance standards.

  • TV & radio broadcast prep
  • Live event sound engineering
  • Loudness compliance (EBU R128 / ATSC A/85)
  • OB (Outside Broadcast) experience

Podcast & Voiceover

High-quality audio for podcasts, audiobooks, corporate narration, and advertising - clear and ready to publish.

  • Voice recording & editing
  • Noise reduction & cleanup
  • Music & SFX integration
  • RSS-ready episode delivery

Live Sound

End-to-end live sound engineering for concerts, corporate events, theatre, houses of worship, and outdoor festivals.

  • FOH & monitor mixing
  • PA system design & setup
  • Stage patching & RF coordination
  • Multitrack recording of live shows
  • Rehearsal & soundcheck management

Post-Production

Complete audio post for film, TV, and digital content - from dialogue editing to final deliverable with all specs met.

  • Dialogue editing & ADR
  • Sound design & Foley
  • Score & music editing
  • Final mix & DCP delivery
